WHAT IS A SMART CLASSROOM "A COMPLETE OVERVIEW"

have you ever heard of a smart class room, or maybe even taught in one? Smart class and smart technology are buzzwords that have been flying in the school for years, but what actually is a smart classroom?

What is a smart classrom: simple DEFINITION

smart classroom is an EdTech-upgraded classroom that enhances the teaching and learning process for both the teachers and the students by inculcating audio, video, animations, images, multimedia etc. This increases the engagement factor and leads to better-performing students

Why is a smart classroom important?

Students are sure to become interested when technology is incorporated into regular lessons. It makes education fun and enjoyable. For example, a teacher can use an engaging video to explain photosynthesis with the aid of smart classroom technology. This approach promotes greater student involvement because smart classrooms can make challenging or uninteresting subjects more engaging. Technology-integrated classrooms fascinate students and build their interest in studies. With the aid of improved learning techniques, they are intrigued by the subjects and comprehend them better. Individuals can retain the information for a longer period of time when they grasp it better.
